# Executive Communication Templates

## Slack DM

Use for quick executive updates or asks.

```text
Bottom line: [what changed + why it matters].

- Status: [Green/Yellow/Red/Blue/Gray] because [reason].
- What changed: [material delta].
- Why it matters: [impact in the recipient's frame].
- Ask: [decision/input/awareness/no action].
```

## Executive Email

Use when the message needs to be forwarded or archived.

```text
Subject: [Outcome or decision needed] - [topic]

Bottom line:
[One sentence with the point.]

Status:
[Label] - [reason.]

What changed:
- [Delta 1.]
- [Delta 2.]
- [Delta 3.]

Why it matters:
[Short paragraph tying facts to business, customer, launch, revenue, risk, quality, or reputation.]

Ask:
[Exact decision, input, or action needed. Say "No action needed" if FYI.]

Risks / watchouts:
- [Risk + owner + mitigation/date.]
- [Open question + owner/date.]
```

## Weekly Leadership Update

Use at most eight bullets total.

```text
[Program / team] update - [date]

Bottom line: [one-sentence overall state].

Done:
- [Outcome, not activity.]
- [Outcome, not activity.]

In progress:
- [Current work + expected decision or milestone.]
- [Current work + expected decision or milestone.]

Risks / decisions:
- [Risk or decision + owner + needed action.]

Ask:
[One sentence. Use "No action needed" if FYI.]
```

## Escalation

Use when normal ownership paths are blocked.

```text
Bottom line: [thing is blocked] and we need [specific executive action] by [date/time].

Impact: [what slips, breaks, costs money, creates risk, or affects customer/launch/reputation].
Root cause: [short factual cause, no blame].
Tried already: [owner/path attempted].
Options: [A/B if useful, with tradeoff].
Ask: [specific action or decision].
```

## Decision Memo

Use for longer context. Keep the main memo to 1-2 pages and move details to appendix.

```text
Decision needed:
[The exact decision.]

Recommendation:
[Answer first.]

Why now:
[Timing pressure or opportunity.]

Options:
- Option A: [benefit, cost, risk.]
- Option B: [benefit, cost, risk.]
- Option C: [benefit, cost, risk.]

Evidence:
- [Data/source 1.]
- [Data/source 2.]
- [Customer/account/source 3.]

Risks and mitigations:
- [Risk + mitigation + owner.]

Decision owner:
[Name or group.]
```

## Audience Lenses

- Product executive: customer impact, adoption, quality, differentiation, timing, tradeoffs.
- Engineering executive: reliability, execution risk, dependencies, incident/load concerns, resourcing, technical debt.
- GTM executive: customer readiness, messaging, field enablement, revenue/account impact, proof points.
- Creative/brand executive: narrative clarity, claims discipline, visual representation, audience authenticity, reputation risk.
- Legal/policy executive: claims, obligations, review state, risk exposure, approval path.
- Finance executive: forecast, spend, margin, budget, pricing, measurable business outcome.
- Board/staff: strategic implication, material risk, decision needed, external visibility, confidence level.

## Rewrite Pattern

Before:

```text
We had a lot of discussion today and the team is working through Windows, socials, and messaging. There are a few things to keep an eye on but overall momentum is good.
```

After:

```text
Codex launch is live across Mac and Windows, and the main creative risk is that current assets still read as Mac-only.

- Status: Yellow because Windows is shipped but underrepresented in launch visuals.
- Why it matters: partners and press may incorrectly read the product as Mac-only.
- Ask: For the next creative wave, balance Mac and Windows representation and avoid claiming next week's onboarding upgrade as shipped today.
```

## Common Fixes

- Replace "wanted to share" with the bottom line.
- Replace "we are working on" with current state and next milestone.
- Replace "lots of momentum" with one concrete signal.
- Replace "some risk" with risk, impact, owner, and mitigation.
- Replace "thoughts?" with a specific ask.
- Remove backstory unless it changes the decision.
